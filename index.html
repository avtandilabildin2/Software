What is Computer Science?

Computers and the Internet are having a great impact on human life and society. Our lives, starting in the second half of the twentieth century, have become computerized "at the speed of light." We now live in times referred to as the computer age, the information age, or the Internet age. The Computer Engineering Department offers a unique opportunity for bright young minds to excel in the areas of computer science, computer software, and computer hardware.

Our Vision

  • To engage our students in projects that help them find jobs worldwide

Our Mission

  • To provide a strong background enabling global career opportunities
  • To instill high moral and ethical values
  • To promote leadership through social activities

  • Excellence in teaching and research
  • Loyalty to ethical standards
